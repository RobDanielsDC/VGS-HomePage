Welcome to
Video Game Sage

A community of fans who celebrate classic video games. Whether you’re a gamer, collector, creator or seller, you can find a home here.

You'll Meet Characters Of All Kinds

The Knight

The noble gamer, competitive and dexterous. Always eager to face a new challenge, a new quest or a new personal best.

The Dragon

Adamantly on guard, the fierce collector keeps a close eye on their stash of games. Keeping them orderly and preserved.

The Alchemist

Creative, resourceful. The brewer burns the midnight oil, using their skills to craft new experiences and share them with the community.

The Merchant

The thrifty seller, aquires rare gems to add to their stock. Striking deals with their trusted customers and helping supply the community.
